Travel Journal in Japan

Hiroshima:

I stayed only one day and two nights in Hiroshima. Despite this, it was a very busy stay and I was able to visit various cool places, like the Genbaku Dome, the Peace Memorial Museum, Miyajima Island and the famous Itsukushima Shrine. Unfortunately, the weather was pretty bad that day, but it was still very enjoyable. I also ate a Hiroshima's traditional Okonomiyaki and at night relaxed in the big onsen at the hotel.

Kyoto:

I stayed two days and three nights at Kyoto. Every place was unbelievably crowded by turists. Some of the most memorable places I went to were the Nishiki market, Fushimi Inari-taisha and Kiyomizu-dera temple.

Mount Fuji:

When I came back to Tokyo at the end of the Golden Week, I went on a bus tour to visit multiple famous spots where you can take pictures of Mount Fuji, like Arakurayama Sengen Park, for example. The weather was clear and perfect for taking photos.
