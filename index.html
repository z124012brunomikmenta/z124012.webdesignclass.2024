About me

Academic Life

I'm Bruno Miksucas Pimenta, a student from Brazil currently studying abroad in Shibaura Institute of Technology. I come from the Pontificial Catholical University of Rio de Janeiro, where I study Computer Science.

Some of the projects I've made at my University:

  • Simple Round Robin process scheduler in C;
  • Programmed a timer function on a microcontroller in Arduino;
  • Ludo game in Java;

The programming language I am most experienced at is C, but I also have a bit of experience with Java, Python and html, which I'm currently learning. Therefore, I worked as an assistant/tutor for the Programming in C class at my home university. It was a really worth experience, because of the feeling of being able to help other people, but it's also an opportunity to review fundamental aspects of programming.
